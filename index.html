BOYCOTT INDIAN PRODUCTS !!

We should boycott Indian products and all other stuff from India—be it TV channels, products, or anything else—to make it clear that we are not someone's puppet. By choosing Deshi Products, we stand up for Bangladesh, support local businesses, and assert our nation's dignity. Every decision to reject Indian stuff is a testament to our independence and unity. Together, we send a strong message that our future and our values are our own.
